Our Detailing Services

At Dylan's Detailing, we offer a range of services to meet the needs of every vehicle. Whether you're looking for a quick clean-up or a full restoration, we have a solution for you.

Service What's Included
Exterior Car Wash Our Exterior Car Wash includes a thorough wash of your car's exterior to remove dirt, grime, and road contaminants. We use high-quality products to ensure your car’s paintwork is protected, leaving it looking fresh. This service is ideal for regular maintenance.
Interior Detailing Interior Detailing includes a deep cleaning of your car’s interior. We clean the carpets, seats, dashboard, and all surfaces to remove dust, dirt, and stains. Our team uses specialized tools to reach all the hard-to-reach places and provide a fresh, clean environment inside your car.
Full Vehicle Detailing Our Full Vehicle Detailing includes both exterior and interior detailing. This comprehensive service restores your car to near-new condition and protects its surfaces for long-lasting shine and cleanliness.
Upholstery Extraction We use advanced extraction techniques to deep clean upholstery, removing stains, dirt, and moisture. This add-on service ensures your seats and fabric are fresh, clean, and free of any odors or contaminants.
Ceramic One-Step Polish This add-on service involves applying a ceramic polish that enhances your vehicle's paintwork. The ceramic coating helps protect the surface from contaminants, adds a deep glossy finish, and offers water-repellent properties. Protects your paint for up to a year.
Glass Polishing Our Glass Polishing service removes stubborn water spots, grime, and contaminants from your vehicle’s windows, leaving them crystal-clear. This improves visibility and enhances the overall aesthetic of your car.
Paint Correction Paint correction involves removing imperfections like swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation from your vehicle's paint. This service restores the paint to its original brilliance, removing imperfections and improving the overall appearance. This also comes with a Ceramic Polish to protect the new work.
Ceramic Coating Ceramic Coating is a protective layer applied to your vehicle’s paint to protect it from dirt, water, UV rays, and other contaminants. It enhances the paint’s shine and makes it easier to clean, while providing long-term protection. This can last up to 9 years if cleaned properly.
